Hong Kong University of Science and Technology

Masters in Science, Electronic Engineering

September 2016 to June 2017

Course List

  1. EESM5000: CMOS VLSI Design

    2. CMOS process and design rules; MOS device electronics; CMOS circuit and logic circuit characterization and performance estimation; VLSI design and verification tools.

  2. EESM5060: Embedded Systems

    3. Trends and design strategies for embedded systems; basic hardware and software components involved in a complex embedded system; design and use of System-on-a-Chip (SoC) in embedded systems.

  3. EESM5060L: Embedded Systems Laboratory

    4. Holistic view of embedded system design using smart phone; Design issues such as testability and power considerations.

  4. EESM5620: Flat Panel Displays

    5. Discussions on various flat-panel display technologies with emphasis on liquid crystal displays; liquid crystal alignment and optics; driving methods; active matrix; bistable displays, plasma display panels. inorganic and organic light emitting diodes physics and devices.

  5. EESM5515: IP Networks

    6. Basic layer model for computer communications; TCP/IP and related protocols; local area networks; advanced topics in unicast and multicast routing, QoS, mobile IP, and security.

  6. EESM5700: Image and Video Signal Processing

    7. Multi-dimensional signals and systems; characteristics of image and video signals; visual perception; multi-dimensional sampling and transforms; image and video enhancement and restoration; image and video compression; coding standards; delivery of visual contents over networks.

  7. EESM5670: Advanced Computer and Networking Architectures

    8. Design and implementation of computer architectures; analysis and comparison of different architectures; fundamental concepts including pipelining, instruction-level parallelism, memory hierarchies, input/output architectures, and multiprocessing; modern issues including networking architecture, storage area networking, and VLSI scaling.

  8. EESM5546: Wireless Communication Systems

    9. Overview of cellular structure and frequency reuse; mobile radio propagation and path loss models; statistical nature of radio channels; coding and time or frequency diversity; spread spectrum CDMA techniques and 3G Systems; OFDM and Wireless LAN standards; fast frequency hopping technology; Bluetooth.

  9. EESM5900: Current and Emerging Technologies in Telecommunications

    10. 5G networks and massive MIMO systems; advanced radio resource management (RRM) for video streaming and networked control applications; cloud computing; smart grid networks; bit coin and security; inter-domain routing; content-centric metworking; software-defined networking (SDN) and congestion controls in data center networks
